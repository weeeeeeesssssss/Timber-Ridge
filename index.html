Neighborhood Updates

Entrance Revitalization

As with last year, we are prioritizing the landscaping at our main entrance to maintain the curb appeal of Timber Ridge. After the larger refresh last year, we will have a small crew clean up the plants and bring in fresh mulch this year. At the next HOA meeting, we will discuss making changes to the landscaping at the entrance.

Road Maintenance Initiative

As we can all see on our daily drives, significant segments of Timber Ridge Drive are falling into disrepair. Boardmembers have completed an assessment of our subdivision roads. This assessment will be sent to Pin Oak Township and Madison County to bring awareness to the current condition of the roadway.

Proposed Covenants Update

As discussed in the last HOA meeting, new covenants and restrictions have been drafted to help protect the long-term property values for every resident in Timber Ridge. Notes from the latest meeting have been collected and incorporated into the proposed C&R's, and these amended documents will be shared ahead of the next HOA meeting.

Seasonal Lawn Tip: Spring 2026

The secret to a lush summer lawn starts now. Applying a pre-emergent herbicide this month is critical for preventing crabgrass and broadleaf weeds before they germinate in the warming soil.

  • • Apply treatment when soil temps reach 55°F
  • • Sharpen mower blades for a cleaner cut
  • • Aerate high-traffic areas if soil is compacted
